## 📌 Project Overview

A complete end-to-end Business Analyst case study analyzing **15,104 orders** from **2,430 unique customers** across **15 cities** and **6 product categories** (Jan–Dec 2026) to identify revenue leakage, customer behaviour patterns, and data-backed growth recommendations.

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| **Tools** | MySQL · Power BI · Microsoft Excel · DAX |
| **Domain** | E-Commerce / Retail |
| **Dataset** | 15,104 Orders · 2,430 Customers |
| **Period** | January – December 2026 |
| **Cities** | 15 Indian Cities |
| **Categories** | 6 Product Categories |

---

## 🔍 Business Problem

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| ✅ Total Revenue (Delivered) | ₹12,78,99,261 |
| ❌ Lost to Cancellations | ₹4,91,43,990 (22.1% cancel rate) |
| ❌ Lost to Returns | ₹4,19,73,244 (20.0% return rate) |
| 🔴 **Total Revenue Loss** | **₹9,11,17,234 (41.6% of potential revenue)** |

---

## 📊 Key Findings

1. **Cancellation rate (22.1%)** is 2x the industry benchmark of ~10%
2. **COD orders** have the highest cancellation rate — shift to prepaid is critical
3. **Electronics** drives ₹9.16Cr revenue but has 19.3% return rate
4. **674 Platinum customers** drive disproportionate revenue — loyalty program needed
5. **Oct–Nov seasonal spike** — festive season inventory planning required
6. **Tier-2 cities** (Surat, Jaipur, Coimbatore) underperforming vs Tier-1

---

## 💡 Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Expected Impact | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Mandatory order confirmation + live delivery ETA | Save ₹1.5–2Cr/year | 🔴 HIGH |
| 2 | ₹29 COD fee OR 5% prepaid discount | Shift 20% COD to prepaid | 🔴 HIGH |
| 3 | Product videos, 360° images, size guides | Reduce returns by 20% | 🔴 HIGH |
| 4 | Electronics comparison tool + spec checklist | Save ₹50–70L | 🔴 HIGH |
| 5 | Platinum loyalty — priority delivery + early access | LTV +25–30% | 🔴 HIGH |
| 6 | Pre-stock Sep; loyalty early access Oct 1 | +15–20% Q4 revenue | 🟡 MEDIUM |
| 7 | City-specific promos for Tier-2 cities | Tier-2 revenue +20% | 🟢 LOW |
